About Linda Kiesel

Linda Kiesel attended college at the University of Delaware, Newark, Delaware, where she received a B.S. in Business Administration, with a concentration in Operations Management in 1984. After an early career as a Computer Engineer with Hewlett Packard and E.I. DuPont Company, she served as CFO at Creative Precision, Inc, an aerospace manufacturing company owned by her husband, Allen. Later, Linda imagined an encore career and attended Widener University Law School, graduating in 2010 with a Juris Doctorate.

Linda Kiesel recently founded AZ Elder Law Group, PLLC, located in Phoenix, Arizona, in order to serve the varied legal needs of the elderly, disabled, and American Veterans. The firm is also involved in specialized estate planning for families with young children.
